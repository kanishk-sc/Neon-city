Welcome to the Neon City

You find yourself standing at the edge of a vibrant neon city that seems to pulse with electric energy. The air hums with the sound of distant music and the glow of countless signs. You have no memory of how you got here, but something tells you this city holds the key to your escape.

The streets stretch before you in multiple directions, each promising different adventures. Your choices will determine your fate in this mysterious urban labyrinth.
