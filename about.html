How to Play

Welcome to "Lost in a Neon City", an interactive choose-your-own-adventure experience! This game puts you in control of a mysterious journey through a vibrant cyberpunk cityscape.

Gameplay Instructions

  1. Read each page carefully to understand your current situation
  2. Choose from the available options by clicking on the links
  3. Your choices will lead you to different locations and outcomes
  4. Some paths may lead back to earlier locations - this is intentional!
  5. Try to find the secret arcade for the ultimate adventure
