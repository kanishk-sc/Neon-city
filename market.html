🏪 The Bustling Market District

You step into a vibrant marketplace where neon signs flicker above rows of stalls. Vendors call out in languages you don't recognize, and the air is thick with the scent of exotic spices and electronic components. Strange artifacts and glowing trinkets are displayed everywhere.

A mysterious merchant in a hooded cloak catches your eye, their stall filled with glowing crystals and ancient-looking devices. Nearby, a dark alley beckons with its shadows, while the main market continues deeper into the district.

Your Options:

  • 🛒 Approach the mysterious merchant
  • 🌑 Investigate the dark alley
  • 🏠 Return to the city entrance

Market Observations:

You notice several interesting details:

  1. The merchant's crystals seem to pulse with inner light
  2. Strange symbols are carved into the alley walls
  3. Some vendors appear to be watching you closely
